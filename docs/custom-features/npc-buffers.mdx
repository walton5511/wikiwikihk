# NPC Buffers

## Description
![](/img/custom-features/npc-buffers/dialogs.png)

**NPC buffers** were introduced to help players in their **PVE gameplay**. There are several Wise Druids distributed around the game, from which you can receive several kind of buffs that are split into 2 categories and certain level brackets.

![](/img/custom-features/npc-buffers/dialogs-price.png)

The 2 categories are "Adventurer Blessing" and "Hero's Blessing", and the difference between them is their price and the buffs you get.

## Adventurer Blessing

This buff pack is for players that need essential blessings. It includes the blessings of Wild Keepers and Holy Mages
