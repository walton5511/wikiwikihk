Tattoos - Guide

What is a Tattoo?‚Äã

First I will give a brief explanation of what a tattoo is as many players have probably never played Act 7. A tattoo is a similar mechanic to a buff, which we can cast whenever we want and it will have a cooldown time, it will grant us new abilities with very useful passives, depending on your playstyle you can try to get the one you like the most.

Where to get a Tattoo?‚Äã

To make a tattoo we will need to have hero level c30 and head to Olorun Village in Moritius. Once we are here we will talk to the NPC Churisgo.

Tattoo Designs‚Äã

Once we talk to Churisgo we will choose the Shop option, where we will find the Tattoo Design section. There are a total of five tattoo designs or tattoo patterns, each of which has a total of 8 tattoos which we can get randomly when we try to "Receive a Tattoo". If any of the tattoos are not to your liking you can remove them at any time with an item called Tattoo Remover, we can buy this item in the Equipment Shop once we talk to Logotor, the price of this item will be 5,000,000 gold.

Tattoo Tips‚Äã

  • Most of the items needed to make a pattern or optimise a tattoo can be obtained from the Spirit King Kirollas box.
  • When trying to optimise a tattoo there will be a percentage of downgrade, to avoid this we can buy a Tattoo Safeguard Scroll from the NPC Logotor for the price of 1,000,000 Gold.
  • You can only have a total of two tattoos, which you can see by clicking on the "K" button in the tattoos section. Tattoos can be placed in the skill bar.
  • If at any time you wish to remove a tattoo you can purchase a Tattoo Remover, we can buy this item in the Equipment Shop once we talk to Logotor, the price of this item will be 5,000,000 Gold.
  • Loa Switch Crystal has been added, which will allow you to change your current tattoo to a random tattoo of any other pattern, we can buy this item in the Equipment Shop once we talk to Logotor, the price of this item will be 100,000,000 Gold. The optimisation of the tattoo WILL NOT GET LOST after changing it with this item.
  • In the same way we have added the Tattoo Switch Scroll which will allow us to change one tattoo for another of the same pattern, we can buy this item in the Equipment Shop once we talk to Logotor, the price of this item will be 100,000,000 Gold. The optimisation of the tattoo WILL NOT GET LOST after changing it with this item.
