Act 7 Questline guide

Guide for the Act 7.1 Main Questline with Mobs, NPC Spots and Quest items.
To start the Act 7.1 Main Questline you have to be lv. 99+30.
If you start with lvl 99+50 you will end up in the end with lvl 99+55

You can get a7 questline from Merchant Ben at Port Alveus Square
